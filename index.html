lostmyname

During the Discord username migration in 2023, a close friend of mine (commonly known as Kakagou12) took the username spgoding. We tried to transfer the username to me but failed. I decided to let them keep the name and used lostmyname.spgoding.com as my own username instead.

Seldomly Asked Questions

Why did Kakagou12 do this?
It was a prank that unfortunately had lasting impact.
Isn't this impersonation?
Technically yes, but I'm not too bothered by it. I just hope they use their newfound stolen identity for the good kind of evil /s. When we tried to transfer the username to me, I waited for at least 8 days but the username never became available to me. We thought someone else might have taken the name but Kakagou12 was able to claim it back. I had no interests in figuring out the exact reservation period set by Discord after a username change and would rather have my friend holding my name than some other rando.
Have you tried …?
No and I'm tired.

It's been at least a year and I don't think so.
